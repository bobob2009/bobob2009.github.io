Venture for America

Venture for America aims to create 100,000 US jobs by 2025 by driving talent toward startup and early stage growth companies in emerging US cities like Detroit, New Orleans, Providence, Cincinnati, Las Vegas, Cleveland, Baltimore, and Philadelphia. VFA seeks visionary companies to partner with us in support of the next generation of young entrepreneurs.
