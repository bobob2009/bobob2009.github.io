Become A Fellow

Gain New Skills

Fellows gain hands-on experience building a business from the ground up. VFA’s educational component gives Fellows the best of business school without the school debt. Training Camp teaches hard skills through a mix of real-life challenges, inspiring guest speakers, and valuable advice.

Gain Access to the VFA Network

The Fellow class consists of exceptional people who will be lifelong partners, advisors, and colleagues. Fellows are connected to leading entrepreneurs and investors in their regions. A great network is one of the main reasons people go to business school. Through VFA, yours is ready-made and much deeper.

Impact the Community

Venture for America fellows become deeply involved in their placement cities. Many fellows join local sports teams, volunteer, and become entrenched in the local entrepreneurship community. The VFA team plans events with local business leaders and supports fellows in all of our cities.
